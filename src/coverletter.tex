%-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
%	LETTER CONTENT
%-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
\begin{cvletter}

  % ── Paragraph 1: Opening ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
  % State the exact role + company. Mention a referral or contact if you have one
  % ("After speaking with {Name} at {event}, I…"). Name something specific about
  % the company — a product, value, or mission detail — not generic praise.
  % End with a fit claim.
  I am a Front-End Engineer with over six years of experience in platform engineering, scalable micro-frontend architecture, and high-performance design systems. I am writing to apply for the <Role Title> position at <Company Name>, which I discovered via <source, e.g.\ LinkedIn / your careers page>. <Company Name>'s work on <specific product, engineering initiative, or stated value> aligns closely with what I find most meaningful in this field, and I believe my background makes me a strong candidate for this role.

  % ── Paragraph 2: Strongest relevant experience ────────────────────────────
  % One or two achievements with metrics, each mapped to a top job requirement.
  % Embed keywords from the job description naturally.
  At Neoxi, I stabilized the release pipeline by building comprehensive end-to-end and visual test suites with Playwright and Cypress, reducing CI minutes by approximately 30\% and cutting production bugs by 30\%. I also redesigned the GitHub Actions pipeline with build caching and automated releases, shortening build times from 12.5 to 8.5 minutes and increasing deployment frequency by 80\%. These improvements directly address <specific requirement from the posting, e.g.\ release reliability, CI/CD ownership, or delivery velocity>.

  % ── Paragraph 3: Supporting experience or company fit ─────────────────────
  % Cover a second dimension of the role, or deepen company-values alignment.
  Earlier at Shop-Ware, I led a phased Backbone-to-React migration that reduced initial page load time by 40\%, and I mentored three mid-level engineers through code review and technical pairing, establishing standards that cut UI regressions by 25\%. I also drove adoption of a Storybook-based component library that accelerated UI delivery by 25\% and improved cross-team design consistency — the kind of platform investment that <Company Name>'s <engineering team / product focus> would benefit from directly.

  % ── Paragraph 4: Closing + call to action ────────────────────────────────
  % Two sentences only. Restate enthusiasm, then request next steps.
  I welcome the opportunity to speak with you further about how my experience aligns with your goals for this role. Thank you for your time and consideration.

\end{cvletter}
